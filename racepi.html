Recipe of Chicken Biryani

Chicken Biryani is a savory chicken and rice dish
that include layers of chicken, rice, and aromatiice that are
steamed together

Ingredients list

  • 1/2 Cup Briyani/Basmati Rice
  • Some Dry Garma Masala
  • Suffered and 3 Glass Water
  • Bring It To Boil
  • Salt 1/2 TBS or Taste
  • (Star Anise)One
  • (Bay Leaves)Two
  • Cooking Oil
  • Mix Well

Method

  1. Suffered Fry Chicken
  2. Add Fried Onion
  3. Add Coriander And Mint
  4. Add Layer of Rice
  5. Add 2 tbsp of Food color
  6. Mix Well
  7. Last serve it
